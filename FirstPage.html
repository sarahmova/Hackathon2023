About Us

Pair'Em is a platform built around the goal of making education more accessible to the general public. As a group of computer science and engineering students at Santa Clara Univeristy, we came together with the intention of making an app that can help people. Considering the lack of accessibility to healthcare, education, and resources in America, we decided upon a platform that increased accessibility all three, with no cost to the user. Pair'Em's purpose is to connect people in need of general advice to undergrad and grad students, majoring in the area of expertice. This offers up-to-date information from credible college students to people looking for free advice. Additionally, it gives students the opportunity to tackle some unbilled practice hours in the industry they will be entering after graduation.
